Our Story

From a single idea to a regional partner.

Founded in Riyadh in 2007, TalentS began with a clear mission: to create experiences that unlock human potential. From early educational programs to today's full-scale museums and visitor centers, our work has grown — but the mission hasn't changed.

Today, we operate across the MENA region with offices in Saudi Arabia and the UAE, collaborating with international partners and trusted by leading cultural and government institutions.

Our teams are multidisciplinary by design — designers, fabricators, educators, producers, and operators all working under one roof. That's how we deliver tailor-made, full turnkey solutions.

The Tālia Experience

Rooted in Saudi Arabia and connected to the world

Tālia is a cultural program designed to create new pathways for learning, exchange, and global understanding, contributing to a more vibrant and globally connected society.

Tālia offers a different kind of cultural experience abroad — one that goes beyond what is seen, into how art, culture, and institutions actually operate.

Instead of moving from one landmark to another, each program provides access to leading cultural institutions, exhibitions, and creative spaces. Museums, galleries, and cultural environments are experienced with context, guidance, and depth.

As part of a structured cultural immersion program, each journey connects people, ideas, and institutions, offering a deeper understanding of global art and cultural ecosystems.

Tālia is part of National Talents Company, supporting the development of cultural professionals and globally curious individuals in Saudi Arabia.

Why Tālia

Direct exposure to international cultural institutions, offering insight into how exhibitions, heritage, and creative practices are developed

A deeper understanding of how artists, institutions, and cultural environments come together across global cities

Engage with curators and cultural practitioners to explore how exhibitions and cultural experiences are created

Understand the creative ecosystem and explore how festivals, cultural initiatives, institutions, and funding models support the global arts and culture sector

Tālia in Rome

Immerse yourself in the eternal city's vast cultural and artistic legacy, from ancient ruins to cutting-edge contemporary spaces. This 6-day program provides a deep, expert-guided exploration of Rome's art scenes, combining historical insight with curatorial expertise.

Itinerary Overview
Welcome briefing, walking tour of MAXXI and contemporary galleries in Flaminio. Evening reception with local curators.
Vatican Museums, the Sistine Chapel, and a private after-hours visit to the Borghese Gallery with a senior curator.
Behind-the-scenes at Fondazione Alda Fendi. Curatorial workshops, networking lunch, closing reception.
Morning reflection session, group photo, and transfer to the airport.
